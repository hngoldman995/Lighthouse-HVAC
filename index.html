Lighthouse HVAC LLC is a family owned full service heating and air conditioning company, serving both residentail and commerical customers. Located in Edgewater Maryland, our goal is to earn your trust and become your first choice for all of your heating and air conditioning service needs. Whether you need furnace repair, AC maintenance, or full system replacement, we’re ready to assist.

Maintenance

Why is maintenance important?

Regular maintenance helps your system produce and distribute hot and cold air more evenly and steadily.

What are the benefits of having maintenance done annually?

Maintenance has been proven to ensure proper function and help reduce the odds that you come home from work one winter’s or summer’s day to an uncomfortable home.
