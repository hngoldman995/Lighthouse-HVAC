Indoor Air Quality

Why it's important

When air inside your home becomes more polluted than the air outside, what can you do? Your HVAC system affects more than just the temperature of your home. We can also use your HVAC system to address issues with indoor air quality (IAQ). At LightHouse HVAC, we offer a variety of tools and guidance to improve the air quality of your home.
There are many ways to improve your indoor air quality using your HVAC system such as:

  • Increase air circulation
  • Schedule regular maintenance for your HVAC system
  • Check your HVAC filters regularly
  • Invest in indoor air quality (IAQ) products

By increasing your air circulation, you can address several air quality issues including presence of dust, polluted air, humidity, or uneven temperatures.
Increased air circulation means that your HVAC filtration system filters the air in your home frequently and successfully, which can help those who suffer from asthma and allergies.
Checking your HVAC filters is imperative to maintaining proper indoor air quality (IAQ). Your HVAC filter's job is to remove dust, allergens, and debris from the air in your home.
As your filter collects these particles, it's normal for your filter to get dirty. The longer a filter stays in your system, the dirtier it will become. If you go too long between changing your filter, it can become clogged, and clogged filters can seriously damage your system.That is why it's important to change your filter regularly.
Scheduling regular maintenance for your HVAC system can also improve your indoor air quality (IAQ). When an HVAC technician services your system, they don't just check for issues with your system, any HVAC maintenance appointment also includes a cleaning.
Sometimes your existing HVAC system can use a little help to address poor air quality issues, if your health and comfort are at risk, then your HVAC partner can add IAQ products to your system.
Invest in HVAC indoor air quality products such as humidifiers, dehumidifiers, and air purifiers if necessary.
